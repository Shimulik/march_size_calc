Gear Builder

Add gear pieces per slot, then hit Run to find optimal builds.

How to use
  1. Select your Scenario and any active bonuses in the sidebar.
  2. For each slot, click + Add Piece and select your gear, add your best regular piece and your best prcent piece.
  3. Click a chip to edit level & rarity, or click ✕ to remove.
  4. Hit Run to compute optimal combinations per base march range.

How to read results
  1. In-game: select your march size preset, then unequip the helmet, chest, pants, boots, ring, and weapon.
  2. Check your base march size from the marching screen.
  3. Find the row whose range contains your march size - that's your best build.
